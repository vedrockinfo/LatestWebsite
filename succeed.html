Succeed After School Program

SUCCEED is an After-school program

SUCCEED is an After-school program providing academic support instead of regular tuition for your kid in the out-of-school time. Synchronous and asynchronous classes are organised for kindergarten preschoolers, elementary and secondary school students with an intent to encourage the children and youth of all ages to improve their academic results without having to run out of home and finding good tutorial help.
